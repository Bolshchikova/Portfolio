Safe Drive Systems landing page

Goal: Design landing page to entice specific categories of users to purchase the devices produced by Safe Drive Systems company.

Process: Search for images in Shutterstock, image manipulation, creating wireframes, design mockups, usability testing.

Skills: Photoshop.

Source

Result

The width of the source image was quite insufficient. It was necessary to increase the width of the image in a full screen.

1. A car of similar size and angle was found in Shutterstock.

2. A new image was transformed to match the maximum possible angle of the source image and new one.

3. Because of the impossibility of exact matches of the source and the new images of a car some items of the source image were erased.

4. The driver and some specific items from the source image were moved to the new image.

5. Color and tonal corrections were made. The final image was obtained.
