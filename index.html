About me

Hi! My name is Olga Bolshchikova and I'm a graphic designer. I have pursued creativity since my childhood.
I graduated from a music school, participated in student theater performances. I have a Bachelor Degree in engineering. After hiring in a publishing house I've decided to self-study design. I've mastered desktop publishing software and have been working as a designer since 2000.
I have always loved to learn, so now I keep developing my skills in print, web and UX/UI design.

 

I strive to make simple and clear design trying to find
harmonious blend of aesthetics and functionality.
Whenever possible I add a storyline and a bit of humor.

 

Besides that I'm caring wife, mom, cat owner, and travel lover as well as passionate knitter and crocheter.

 

Thank you for taking time to look around!
