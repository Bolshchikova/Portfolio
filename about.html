Hello! I'm Olga Bolshchikova

I am a UX designer, keenly informed by educational and professional experiences in engineering, visual design and psychology. I believe the "less is more" approach is key when it comes to a strong successful design. I find Albert Einstein's aphorism  inspirational:

"Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler."

I am never afraid to say "I don't know". On the contrary, I think that a willingness to admit this just opens the doors for more opportunities and experiences. Staying open-minded and getting insights from individuals much smarter than myself is what shapes me into the designer I am today. Pushing myself through challenges and constantly learning is what I value most.

And I Do Other Things

I am a musician: guitar, piano and voice. Most of my life I sang in Russian, now am learning to sing in English.

I am a crafter. Knitting and crochet help me to be very detail-oriented. I love creating lacy and exquisite items and detailed toys. You can see them on my website NiftyKnits.

I am a world traveler. My husband and I have visited 20 countries, 103 cities (and counting!). One highlight: hiking the Yosemite Falls Trail, North America’s tallest waterfall.
