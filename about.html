In a nutshell

My Story

My name is Olga Bolshchikova, and I am a Visual and UI/UX designer, keenly informed by educational and professional experiences in engineering, visual design and psychology. I am equal parts logical, analytical, and visual.

 

I believe the "less is more" approach is key when it comes to a strong successful design. The user's attention span is narrow, and navigating through the maze of advertising can be a challenge. Organization, legibility, and versatility have great importance for good design. I strive to join these elements together to construct designs coupled with harmony that work in the real world.

 

I am never afraid to say "I don't know". On the contrary, I think that a willingness to admit this just opens the doors for more opportunities and experiences. Staying open-minded and getting insights from individuals much smarter than myself is what shapes me into the designer I hope to become. I am tireless in my desire to learn and improve on what I already know.

 

And I Do Other Things

  • I am a musician: guitar, piano and voice.
  • I am an artisan. Knitting and crocheting helps me to be very detail-oriented. I love to create lacy and exquisite items and detailed toys. You can see more items on my hobby-website NiftyKnits.
  • I am a world traveler. My husband and I have visited 20 countries, 103 cities (and counting!).
  • One highlight: hiking the Yosemite Falls Trail, North America’s tallest waterfall.
