The Menace of Unreality

special report

Well-known journalists Michael Weiss and  Peter Pomerantsev conducted journalistic research and wrote a report titled "The Menace of Unreality: How the Kremlin Weaponizes Information, Culture, and Money". I was responsible for creating a multi-page booklet of the report to presenting it at a conference in London, and at a political discussion forum in Washington, D.C.
