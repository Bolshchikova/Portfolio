Graphic Design

Ayla Networks

Ayla Networks provides an Agile IoT Platform, accelerating the development, support, and ongoing enhancements of connected products for the Internet of Things. As part of BlueApple Design team, I was responsible for creating e-Books and brochures by following corporate brand identity and visual guidelines. My role included graphic design and layout, creating interactive PDF-file and web banners.

Safe Drive Systems

To increase the product sales, SDS decided to cooperate with dealerships. I was responsible for design, layout and print production of the flyers with special coupons that were distributed in dealerships. In cooperation with over 50 dealerships, SDS has increased sales more than doubled in three months.

Ehang

EHANG, Inc. is a technological innovation company specializing in R&D, manufacturing and sales of the personal Autonomous Aerial Vehicle EHANG 184 and GhostDrone. I designed a series of posters that were placed in Best Buy and Fry's stores as part of EHANG advertising campaign. The challenge was to make up attractive and fun compositions with a bit of humor.
