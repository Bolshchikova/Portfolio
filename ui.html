UI Design

Safe Drive Systems

Safe Drive Systems is a developer and distributor of collision avoidance and lane departure warning systems. SDS mission is to keep a car driver and their family safe on the road.

 

Landing Page

I designed a responsive landing page whose purpose was to encourage car drivers to purchase an anti-collision radar system for their family members.

Blog

As part of the website revamping, I was responsible for the SDS blog redesign to make it more modern and human-friendly.

 

Here is their previous design.

Before diving into the project, I asked the stakeholders some questions to keep everybody on the same page:

  • What are we trying to achieve with the redesign?
  • What are the problems of the current design?
  • What are the must-haves?
  • Are there missing features that need to be included?

 

I started by going through the current blog and listed things that could be improved. Various issues raised for the SDS blog included poor copy readability and legibility (e.g. long line lengths). Inconsistencies in layout and typography made the pages look disjointed.

 

Final Design

I created a more structured layout and hierarchy. I moved the share buttons and social media to the top-right for better users' interaction. Also, I added the comment section and changed the color of the CTA-button. Now all pages look more visually pleasing than before and have a streamlined finish.

Conclusion

The entire website conversion has increased by 100-150% (by number of leads and sales). Blog traffic has increased by over 50% in 1 month, also the blog has enabled the start of advertising through Outbrain and Taboola.
