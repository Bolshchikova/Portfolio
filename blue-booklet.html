Poultry Breeding Plant's booklet

A presentation booklet of the Joint Stock Company Poultry Breeding Plant “Sverdlovsky” for an international poultry exhibition

Goal:  Create a booklet for international exhibition and represent the Plant as Magicland, where kind wizards create and breed purebred chickens.

Process: Brainstorming, concept design, creating collages, making layout, preparing files for print.

Skills: InDesign, Photoshop, Acrobat.
